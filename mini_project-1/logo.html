Course Operations

The course provides basic and advanced knowledge on how the World Wide Web works, related network protocols, and the process of developing a complete web application from Front-end to Back-end.

Opening Ceremony

The class will start in the second week of the semester. Students need to prepare personal computers with basic development environments such as Node.js, VS Code installed.

Seminar Information

Seminar sessions will be held periodically in the middle of the semester with the participation of guest speakers from major technology companies, sharing about modern Web programming trends.

Interested Companies

We cooperate with many IT companies to bring internship and job opportunities to students who achieve excellent results in this course.
